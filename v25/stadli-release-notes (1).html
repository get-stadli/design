Unified Fan Timeline

Tickets, emails, surveys, and merch now live on one scrollable timeline per fan — every touchpoint in the order it happened.

Predictive Sell-Out

A new model forecasts your sell-out date and recommends when to release held inventory — before the demand curve turns.

Segment Builder v2

Compose audiences from any mix of behaviour, spend, and attendance, with a live count that updates as you add each rule.

● Improved

Faster checkout

Ticket checkout is about 40% faster on mobile, with Apple Pay and Google Pay moved to the top of the flow.

 

Bulk email scheduling

Schedule up to 50 campaigns at once and stagger send windows by the recipient’s local timezone.

 

Smarter CRM import mapping

Column mapping now auto-detects common fields and remembers the layout from your last import.

● Fixed
—

Survey responses could be double-counted in analytics exports. Counts are now reconciled at export time.
—

Recurring event reminders drifted by an hour across daylight-saving changes. Reminders now pin to the event’s timezone.
—

Revenue rounding was inconsistent in multi-currency reports. Totals now round once, at the report level.

Move to v2 before the cutoff to keep exports running. The migration guide maps every v1 endpoint to its replacement.
